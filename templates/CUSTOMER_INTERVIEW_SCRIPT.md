# Customer Interview Script

## Interview Goal

Understand the customer's actual behavior, pain, alternatives, buying process, and urgency.

## Opening

Thank you for speaking with us. This is not a sales call. We are trying to understand how you currently handle this problem and what makes it difficult.

## Screening Questions

- What is your role?
- What type of company or team do you work in?
- Are you involved in solving or buying solutions for this problem?

## Problem Questions

- When did this problem last happen?
- What triggered it?
- What did you do next?
- How long did it take to resolve?
- What did it cost in time, money, risk, or lost opportunity?

## Current Alternative Questions

- What tools, people, or processes do you use today?
- What works well?
- What is frustrating or insufficient?
- Have you paid for anything to solve this?

## Buying and Priority Questions

- Who owns this problem internally?
- Who controls the budget?
- What would make this urgent?
- What would cause you to switch from your current approach?

## Close

- Who else should we speak with?
- May we follow up with a prototype, offer, or pilot?

## Interviewer Notes

Avoid pitching the solution until the end. Record exact phrases when the customer describes pain, urgency, workarounds, or budget.
