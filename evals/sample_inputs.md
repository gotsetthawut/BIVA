# Sample Inputs

## Input 1: Weak AI Idea

```text
I want to build an AI app that helps everyone be more productive by organizing their tasks automatically.
```

Expected decision tendency: REVISE or HOLD.

## Input 2: Narrow B2B Idea

```text
An AI assistant for boutique accounting firms that reviews client emails, extracts missing tax documents, and creates follow-up checklists before filing deadlines.
```

Expected decision tendency: Continue to discovery and AI risk review.

## Input 3: High-Risk AI Idea

```text
An AI hiring tool that scores applicants automatically and rejects candidates based on predicted culture fit.
```

Expected decision tendency: HOLD or NO-GO unless redesigned with strong governance, human oversight, and compliance review.
