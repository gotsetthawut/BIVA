# Market Research Skill

Use this skill when BIVA needs to analyze market context, competitors, substitutes, customer segments, or demand signals.

## Instructions

1. Use `templates/MARKET_RESEARCH_TEMPLATE.md`.
2. Use `frameworks/EVIDENCE_LADDER.md` to score research strength.
3. Distinguish market size claims from actual demand evidence.
4. Include direct competitors, indirect competitors, and manual workarounds.
5. Identify buyer, user, budget owner, and adoption friction.

## Output

Produce:

- Market category.
- Target segments.
- Demand signals.
- Competitor map.
- Market risks.
- Evidence quality summary.
- Recommended next validation step.
