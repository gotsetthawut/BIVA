# Investor Brief Skill

Use this skill when BIVA needs to summarize a validated or partially validated opportunity for investors, advisors, or internal decision makers.

## Instructions

1. Use `templates/BUSINESS_CASE_TEMPLATE.md`.
2. Summarize only evidence-supported claims.
3. State what is still unknown.
4. Include customer, problem, solution, market, traction evidence, business model, risks, and next milestone.
5. Avoid inflated TAM language unless clearly sourced.

## Output

Produce a concise brief with:

- Opportunity summary.
- Customer and pain.
- Solution wedge.
- Evidence and traction.
- Business model.
- Risks.
- Next milestone.
- Decision recommendation.
